How To Write a Successful Story ?

To write a good story to be able make your project more appealing is an important key to success in crowdfunding. Don't forget to follow these tips to write a good crowdfunding story!

1Include an appealing title!

  • Make your title to perfectly describe your project
  • Make it understandable and concise!

2Pictures are needed!

  • Include pictures that help you describe better your project
  • Better to include high quality pics made by you!
  • Keep your best picture for the cover!

3Answer essential questions in your story

  • WHO are you?
  • WHERE do you live?
  • WHAT problem you want to solve?
  • WHY do you need that amount of funds?
  • WHEN do you need the funds?
  • HOW are you going to use the collected funds?

4The more, the better!

  • Include any extra information that might help other to understand your cause!
  • Include your social networks, web, etc.
